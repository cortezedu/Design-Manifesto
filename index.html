Focused Content

Having content that is focused on one thing makes it easier for the user to direct their attention to one piece of information at a time. Use as much space as needed to make the webpage look as clean as possible. Aesthetically pleasing is the goal.

Bootstrap

Using Bootstrap makes designing easier to do. Make use of the Bootstrap classes for padding, margins, color, font-weight, etc. There are many components at your fingertips including badges, cards, dropdowns, navbars, and more!

Grids

Use grids to organize content with responsive design in mind. Choose how to layout elements on the grid based on the size of the window. With larger windows, have multiple columns. With smaller windows, take up the entire row.

Fontawesome

Need to add professional icons to your website? Look no further than Fontawesome. You can find many free icons to use. The pro version offers up many more icons to choose from.

Color

Use bold solid colors to make things pop. Have purpose with the colors you choose. Choose contrasting colors for the background and text for easy readability.

Design

Keep the design simple. Use no more than two fonts. Choose fonts that are easy to read like sans-serif fonts. See examples of aesthetically pleasing designs below.
